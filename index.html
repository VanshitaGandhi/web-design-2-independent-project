Yes, you can call me Vee.

I believe great designs have a life of their own. They have the power to generate emotions in us humans and interact with us like a living being. My love of dreamy clouds, the unwavering ocean, earthy grass and moving poetry drives me to create these designs which speak to anyone who sees them. I am blessed to hold this skill and try to make the best of it by helping brands with incredible potential find the right visual representation for the stories they hope to tell.

About me

Hello, I am Vanshita Gandhi. I am a Graphic Design Student at Algonquin College in Ottawa, Canada. I am skilled in the Adobe Suite, including Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign and also possess functional knowledge of coding in HTML and CSS. This portfolio includes glimpses of some of my notable works. In addition to designing, I am also passionate about fine art and photography.
